<p><p>The food texture market encompasses various functional ingredients that enhance the mouthfeel and consistency of food products. Thickeners increase viscosity, providing a rich texture in sauces and soups. Gelling agents create structured, gel-like forms in desserts and spreads. Emulsifiers aid in blending oil and water, stabilizing dressings and creams. Stabilizers maintain the uniformity of textures in products like yogurt and ice cream, while the "Other" category includes ingredients that contribute to texture but don't fit neatly into the first four types.</p></p>
