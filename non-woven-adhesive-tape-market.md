<p><p>The non-woven adhesive tape market comprises three main types: acrylic, rubber, and silicone. Acrylic-based tapes offer strong adhesion and excellent UV resistance, making them ideal for outdoor applications. Rubber tapes provide superior initial tack and conformability, suitable for general-purpose bonding. Silicone tapes excel in high-temperature environments, maintaining performance without leaving residue. Each type serves distinct applications across industries, including automotive, construction, and medical, catering to specific bonding needs based on their unique properties and performance characteristics.</p></p>
