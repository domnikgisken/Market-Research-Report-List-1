<p><strong>The Small Size Panel Display Driver IC Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Source Drive IC</li><li>Gate Drive IC</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The small size panel display driver IC market is segmented into two main types: source drive ICs and gate drive ICs. Source drive ICs manage the voltage and current supplied to the pixels, ensuring accurate color and brightness levels for optimal image quality. In contrast, gate drive ICs control the timing and sequencing of signals that drive the display's rows, facilitating dynamic refresh rates and image stability. Together, they form the backbone of advanced display technologies in compact devices.</p></p>
