<p><p>The plasma treater market consists of two primary types: ground type and portable plasma treaters. Ground type plasma treaters are stationary systems used in industrial settings for surface modification, improving adhesion, and cleaning materials on a larger scale. They typically offer higher power outputs and larger treatment areas. In contrast, portable plasma treaters are compact and versatile, designed for smaller applications and field use. They provide flexibility for on-site treatment, catering to diverse industries that require mobility and ease of operation.</p></p>
