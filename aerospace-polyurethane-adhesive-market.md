<p><p>The aerospace polyurethane adhesive market primarily consists of two types: polyisocyanate and polyurethane. Polyisocyanate adhesives are noted for their excellent adhesion and durability, making them suitable for demanding aerospace applications. In contrast, polyurethane adhesives provide enhanced flexibility and impact resistance, making them ideal for diverse bonding needs across various substrates. Both types are critical in ensuring structural integrity and performance in aerospace components, contributing to the overall safety and efficiency of aircraft operations.</p></p>
