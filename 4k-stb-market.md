<p><p>The 4K STB market encompasses various types, including IPTV, Satellite, DTT, OTT, and Hybrid. IPTV delivers content via internet protocol, ensuring high-quality streaming. Satellite STBs utilize satellite signals for broadcasting high-definition channels. DTT (Digital Terrestrial Television) transmits digital signals over the air, allowing access to local stations. OTT (Over-The-Top) services stream content directly over the internet, bypassing traditional broadcasting. Hybrid STBs combine multiple technologies, offering users flexibility by integrating both broadcast and internet options for seamless viewing.</p></p>
