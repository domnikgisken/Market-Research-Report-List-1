<p><p>The Mercury Porosimeter market is categorized into three types based on pressure levels: low-pressure, medium-pressure, and high-pressure equipment. Low-pressure porosimeters are used for materials with accessible pores, enabling detailed analyses of pore size and distribution. Medium-pressure units offer a balance, suitable for various porous materials, while high-pressure porosimeters are designed for dense or hard samples, providing high-resolution data on complex pore structures. Each type caters to specific research and industrial applications, ensuring accurate material characterization.</p></p>
